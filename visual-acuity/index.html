Test setup

Choose what you'd like to test.

Test type
1Prop your phone upright against the wall behind you, screen facing out toward the mirror.
2Stand 2 m (6 ft) from a large mirror, facing it, phone wall behind you.
3Read the letters in the mirror reflection. Keep your head still.
Effective optical distance: ~4 m (13 ft)  ·  True distance vision test
Near vision test. Hold your device at comfortable reading distance — about 40 cm (16 inches). When the test begins, keep it at that distance and cover one eye at a time.
⚠️ Nearsighted without glasses? Testing at arm's length won't detect myopia — your near focus compensates. Use Mirror Mode for a true distance check, or test corrected vs. uncorrected to estimate your prescription.
