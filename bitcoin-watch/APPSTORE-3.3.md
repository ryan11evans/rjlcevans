# TapBTC 3.3 — App Store copy

Users are jumping from 3.0 → 3.3, so these notes cover everything added since
the approved 3.0 build (portfolio, currencies, new alerts, Face ID, etc.).

---

## "What's New" (release notes) — primary

TapBTC is now a full Bitcoin companion — not just a price ticker.

• Portfolio tracking — enter your Bitcoin and buy price to see live value and
  profit/loss, on your Home Screen, Apple Watch, and in a daily briefing.
• More alerts — new all-time high alerts, halving-countdown milestones, and
  big-move (volatility) alerts, all delivered even when the app is closed.
• Choose your currency — USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, or JPY throughout the app.
• Longer charts — 90D, 1Y, and All-time, with high/low markers.
• Face ID lock — keep your holdings private.
• Denominate in sats, a daily morning briefing, and more.

No account. No ads. Just Bitcoin.
