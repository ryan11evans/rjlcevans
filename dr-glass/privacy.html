Privacy Policy — Dr. Glass

Last updated: May 2026

Dr. Glass is a game created by Dr. Ryan Evans, OD. We respect your privacy. In short: the game collects nothing about you.

No Data Collection

Dr. Glass does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts, no logins, and no tracking of any kind.

On-Device Storage Only

Your game progress, coins, glasses, achievements, and settings are saved only on your own device using local storage. This information never leaves your device and is never sent to us or to any third party.

No Advertising or Analytics

The game contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking software or SDKs.

No Internet Required

Dr. Glass is fully playable offline and does not require a network connection to function.

Children's Privacy

Dr. Glass is suitable for all ages. Because the game collects no data whatsoever, it collects no data from children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by virtue of collecting no personal information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact Dr. Ryan Evans, OD, through rjlcevans.com.
