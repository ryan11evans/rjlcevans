How to Play

Dr. Glass runs forward on his own — you just handle the jumps!

👆
Tap anywhere to jump.
✌️
Tap again in the air for a double jump.
👟
Land on enemies to stomp them.
🧱
Hit a wall to vault off it backward.
💨
Dodge the air puffs — jump over or duck under.
👁️
Grab coins to buy fancy glasses in the shop.

Privacy Policy

Dr. Glass respects your privacy. In short: the game collects nothing about you.

No data collection. Dr. Glass does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts, no logins, and no tracking.

On-device only. Your game progress, coins, glasses, and settings are saved only on your device. This information never leaves your phone and is not sent to us or anyone else.

No ads or analytics. The game contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking software.

No internet required. Dr. Glass is fully playable offline.

Children's privacy. Because the game collects no data at all, it collects no data from children.

If you have any questions, contact Dr. Ryan Evans, OD, via rjlcevans.com.
