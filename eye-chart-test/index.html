Why this one is different

Built by Dr. Ryan Evans, OD

Most vision apps show a chart at an arbitrary size held at phone length — neither of which is how a real exam works. This one calibrates to your exact screen's pixel density and tests at 12 feet using mirror mode, eliminating the inaccuracy you get from holding the chart too close. Every letter is the mathematically correct size for the distance, just like an exam room.

Picks your exact iPhone or iPad model and calculates the precise pixel-per-mm ratio. No guessing.
